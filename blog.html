EMBRACE YOUR TALENT — with Spade.

Hobbies help everybody to enhance themselves in the best possible way and one day these hobbies become our passion, let this passion become a reason to bring out the hidden talent from inside you.

Every Human being is innovative and different in their way, thus this difference leads to a unique talent. Student Organization SPADE with its online contest "SPADE TALENT SHOW" encourages creative minds to showcase their hidden talent

Description of the events are as follows:

  • 01

    Singing

    In a singing competition, we'll try to gather maximum participation to demonstrate their singing skills to a jury and a public audience through an online platform. A singing competition is an excellent arena for testing and perfecting a singer’s skills. A singing competition can also have a major positive impact on the career of a young singer. It is a golden chance for all the music lovers who would like to participate in singing competitions, as the participation is from home.

    Description: The First Round, is the round in which all the participants have to sing songs of their choice. Even those who are not singers but are interested in singing can also participate because we believe in digging out the hidden talent. In this round participants have to sing any song of his/her choice with or without music track and will be evaluated based on LIKE, COMMENTS and SHARE. For participating, students have to record their song by mobile video and have sent it to the following email address.

    Email:

    The time limit of music video or audio will be between 30 seconds to 1 minute. The participants who are selected based on LIKE, COMMENT AND SHARE will enter the second round that will be evaluated by the JUDGES. Winners will be provided Online Goodies.
